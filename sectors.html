Economic & Financial Services

The Ndola Chamber of Commerce supports companies in the Economic & Financial Services sector by facilitating networking, offering industry insights, and promoting collaboration. They help companies access financial resources, navigate regulatory frameworks, and stay updated on economic trends, ultimately fostering a thriving and resilient financial services environment.

Agriculture

The Ndola Chamber of Commerce plays a vital role in the Agriculture sector by connecting farmers, agribusinesses, and related service providers. They facilitate knowledge-sharing, market access, and advocate for policies that enhance agricultural productivity, contributing to the growth and sustainability of the agricultural industry in the region.

Mining and Heavy Duty

In the Mining and Heavy Duty sector, the Ndola Chamber of Commerce acts as a catalyst for growth. They connect mining companies and heavy-duty equipment providers, foster safety and environmental standards, and advocate for policies that support sustainable resource extraction. Their efforts contribute to responsible mining practices and economic development in the region.

Small & Medium Enterprise

For Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs), the Ndola Chamber of Commerce serves as a valuable resource. They offer support in areas like business development, access to markets, and regulatory compliance. By providing training and networking opportunities, they empower SMEs to grow, innovate, and contribute to the local economy's dynamism.

Transport & Logistics

In the Transport & Logistics sector, the Ndola Chamber of Commerce plays a pivotal role. They facilitate connections among transportation companies, logistics providers, and relevant stakeholders. By promoting efficient supply chain solutions and advocating for infrastructure improvements, they help streamline the movement of goods and contribute to regional economic development.

Tourism

Their efforts contribute to increased tourism, job creation, and economic development in the area.
